About the Website

This website helps you calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) and provides a BMI chart for reference. Understanding your BMI can help you assess your body weight in relation to your height and determine if you are in a healthy weight range.

BMI Chart

BMI Category BMI Range
Underweight Less than 18.5
Normal weight 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight 25 - 29.9
Obesity 30 or greater
